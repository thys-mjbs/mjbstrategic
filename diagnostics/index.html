What the diagnostic work covers

Each engagement at MJB Strategic is a focused financial review of how a business behaves beneath surface reporting. The work is modular. You engage for the areas relevant to your situation.

Some engagements are defensive identifying pressure before it compounds. Others are proactive identifying leverage before capital is committed. The domains below represent the core areas of analysis.

1) Profit Driver & Margin Concentration Analysis

This review isolates where real absolute profit is generated. It examines how profit is distributed across products, contracts, customers, or segments, and identifies where margin and scale intersect meaningfully.

In stressed environments, this reveals where margin must be protected. In stable environments, it shows where incremental focus produces disproportionate uplift.

2) Cost Structure & Economic Restructuring Review

This review evaluates whether the cost base supports or constrains profit drivers. It identifies structural cost commitments, hidden inefficiencies, and areas where cost positioning weakens competitiveness.

Under pressure, it clarifies where cost strain originates. Under growth, it highlights where restructuring strengthens long-term margin resilience.

3) Balance Sheet Integrity, Capital Position & Asset Utilisation Review

This review assesses whether assets generate proportional economic return. It identifies idle or underperforming assets and evaluates alternative use, redeployment, or monetisation.

In constrained situations, it exposes capital trapped in low-return holdings. In opportunity-driven situations, it reveals where capital can be redeployed for stronger yield.

4) Cash Flow, Working Capital & Liquidity Analysis

This review reconciles accounting profit with real cash movement. It identifies working capital pressure points, liquidity vulnerability, and dependency on external funding.

Under strain, it clarifies survival timelines and funding exposure. Under growth, it models capital requirements before expansion.

5) Revenue Concentration & Demand Dependency Review

This review assesses exposure to specific customers, segments, or revenue channels. It examines demand durability and sensitivity to shifts in buyer behaviour.

In volatile environments, it quantifies revenue fragility. In stable markets, it highlights diversification opportunity and risk-adjusted expansion paths.

6) Supply Chain Leverage & Control Analysis

This review evaluates dependency on dominant suppliers and upstream vulnerability. It assesses negotiating leverage and feasibility of strengthening sourcing position.

In constrained settings, it reveals margin pressure driven by supplier dominance. In opportunity settings, it identifies where deeper participation improves economics.

7) Forward Financial Modeling & Scenario Testing

This review stress-tests the current structure under realistic adverse and growth scenarios. It quantifies margin shifts, liquidity durability, and capital strain under expansion or contraction.

In defensive cases, it clarifies structural breakpoints. In proactive cases, it supports informed strategic commitment.

Scope and engagement framing

Engagements are defined around specific needs. Some clients require a single focused diagnostic. Others combine multiple domains for broader structural clarity.

Scope is agreed upfront. Fees are quote-based. Focused engagements begin at $2,500. The work is analytical and descriptive. Execution remains with the business owner and their advisors.
