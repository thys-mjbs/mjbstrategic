You can describe the pressure you are seeing or the decision you are preparing for. From there, we determine whether a focused diagnostic is appropriate, and which domains are relevant.

No preparation is required, and you do not need to share sensitive information at this stage. If the situation is a fit, scope and delivery format are agreed upfront. Focused diagnostics begin at $2,500.
