Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you visit the Matthys Creative website. The purpose of this page is to provide clear and transparent disclosure without overstating data collection or compliance.

Website Infrastructure

This website is hosted using GitHub Pages and delivered through Cloudflare. These services are used solely to store and serve the site’s content and to ensure basic performance and availability.

Matthys Creative does not operate its own servers or custom backend systems.

Information Collection

At present, Matthys Creative does not intentionally collect personal information from visitors. In the future, email addresses may be collected voluntarily for newsletters, updates, or notifications related to new writing and releases.

No sensitive personal data is intentionally collected at any time.

Use of Information

If email addresses are collected, they will be used solely for communication related to the studio’s creative work, such as updates or announcements. Information will not be sold or shared for unrelated purposes.

Third-Party Services

This site may reference or link to third-party services, such as Amazon, which operate under their own privacy policies. Matthys Creative has no control over how third-party platforms collect or process data.

Cookies and Tracking

Matthys Creative does not intentionally set tracking cookies or use analytics tools to monitor individual visitors.

Infrastructure providers such as GitHub Pages or Cloudflare may use essential cookies or technical measures that are outside the direct control of Matthys Creative.

User Rights

Where applicable, users may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information that has been voluntarily provided. Requests will be handled in a reasonable manner based on the information available.
