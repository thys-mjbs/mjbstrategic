The problem this addresses

Pressure inside a business is rarely random. It usually comes from interaction between moving parts that are not being viewed together.

Product mix affects margin. Margin affects working capital. Working capital affects liquidity. Supplier terms affect leverage. Revenue concentration affects stability.

When those connections are not clear, owners either operate defensively, or pursue growth without quantifying the capital strain that comes with it.

What this is (and is not)

This is a diagnostic engagement. It is not implementation, outsourced management, or open-ended consulting.

You engage for one defined review area or multiple, depending on where pressure or opportunity exists. Scope is agreed upfront. Delivery is a written report, a walkthrough session, or both.

The output is a clear explanation of what is happening, why it is happening, and what it implies. Decisions and execution remain with you and your existing advisors.

How it works

The process is simple and scoped. The aim is fast clarity, not a long programme.

  • Step 1: Short intake to define the decision, the pressure point, or the opportunity
  • Step 2: Data review and diagnostic analysis across the relevant domains
  • Step 3: Delivery as a written report, a walkthrough, or both

Focused diagnostics begin at $2,500.

Who this is for

Owner-led businesses operating at meaningful scale, where complexity has outgrown intuitive oversight.

This is for operators who want precision before committing capital, changing structure, or pushing growth.

Some clients arrive under pressure. Some arrive preparing for opportunity. The common requirement is the same: clarity before action.

What you receive

A structured diagnostic output grounded in real data and operating reality.

  • Isolation of true profit drivers and margin concentration
  • Cash conversion and working capital timing analysis
  • Capital and asset utilisation review
  • Revenue dependency and demand sensitivity assessment
  • Supplier leverage and upstream pressure mapping
  • Scenario testing under growth and downside cases

The goal is not generic advice. The goal is a clean view of what is driving outcomes, where risk accumulates, and where opportunity sits.
