Operational and financial clarity

When cash stays tight, stock absorbs energy, or growth creates pressure, the issue is rarely effort or intent. It is usually structural.

MJB Strategic produces fixed-scope diagnostic reviews that explain what is happening inside a business, why it feels the way it does, and where pressure is actually coming from.

Get in touch How this works

The problem this addresses

Many owner-managed businesses are operationally active and commercially viable, yet remain under constant financial and operational strain.

Cash flow feels unpredictable. Inventory ties up capital. Margins look acceptable on paper but fail to convert into breathing room. Decision-making becomes reactive because the underlying causes are unclear.

These symptoms are often treated individually. The structure creating them is not.

What this is (and is not)

This is a diagnostic engagement. It is not advisory, implementation, or ongoing consulting.

Each engagement produces a written report based on historical data and existing operational information. The goal is to surface structural pressure points, not to prescribe actions or manage outcomes.

Responsibility for decisions and execution remains with the business owner and their existing advisors.

Who this is for

This work is designed for owner-managed businesses that are trading, growing, and operationally active, but experiencing persistent strain in cash flow, working capital, inventory, or margins.

It is most useful where the business is “busy but tight” — where effort is high, activity is visible, yet relief never quite arrives.

What you receive

A clear, plain-language diagnostic report that explains:

  • What is happening inside the business
  • Why pressure concentrates where it does
  • How operational and financial elements interact

The output is descriptive, not prescriptive. Its value lies in clarity, not instruction.

Why this approach

Most pressure inside a business is structural before it is tactical. Until those structures are visible and understood, changes tend to move effort around rather than relieve it.

A bounded diagnostic creates shared understanding, reduces noise, and provides a stable base for better decisions — whether changes follow or not.
