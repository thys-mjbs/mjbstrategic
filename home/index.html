Operational and financial clarity
for owner-managed businesses

Some businesses come to this work because pressure is building. Others come because they see opportunity and want to move with precision.

In both cases, the issue is the same: the economic structure of the business is not fully visible. Cash feels tighter than it should. Margins move without clear explanation. Growth absorbs more capital than expected. Or opportunity exists, but the leverage points are unclear.

MJB Strategic provides focused financial diagnostics that surface both risk and opportunity inside the current structure before major decisions are made.

Get in touch What to expect

The problem this addresses

Strain inside a business is rarely random. It reflects interaction between margin, capital, inventory, supplier terms, and revenue concentration.

When these dynamics are not clearly mapped, owners either operate defensively or pursue growth without understanding the capital strain involved. Clarity is required in both situations.

What this is (and is not)

This is a financial diagnostic engagement. It is not implementation. It is not management consulting. It is not outsourced execution.

You engage for one defined review area or multiple, depending on where pressure or opportunity exists. Scope is tailored. Engagements are quote-based. Focused diagnostics begin at $2,500.

The objective is structural understanding. Decisions and execution remain with the business owner and their advisors.

Who this is for

Owner-managed businesses operating at meaningful scale. Commercially viable, structurally complex, and beyond purely intuitive financial oversight.

Some are navigating constraint. Some are preparing for expansion. Some want confirmation before committing capital. All require precision.

What you receive

A structured diagnostic output grounded in real data and operating reality.

  • Isolation of true profit drivers
  • Capital utilisation analysis
  • Cash conversion mapping
  • Revenue dependency review
  • Supplier leverage assessment
  • Scenario modelling under growth or contraction

Delivery format is defined upfront written report, walkthrough session, or both. The work identifies where risk accumulates and where opportunity concentrates.

Why this approach

Most businesses do not lack effort. They lack structural clarity.

When economic drivers are isolated, when capital strain is quantified, and when growth impact is modelled, decision quality improves immediately.

In stressed situations, clarity prevents escalation. In stable situations, clarity exposes leverage. Either way, it reduces blind risk and strengthens control.
