Profit Concentration Analyzer

Businesses often review revenue by segment, but revenue alone rarely reveals where profit is actually generated. Different products, services, or customer groups frequently produce very different margins. As a result, a large share of total profit may originate from only a small portion of the business.

Operators typically encounter this issue when a seemingly diversified company suddenly experiences margin pressure after a specific product line weakens or a major customer reduces orders. In many cases the underlying cause is profit concentration: the business relies heavily on a narrow set of activities to generate most of its earnings.

This diagnostic estimates the absolute profit contribution generated by each revenue segment and evaluates how much of the total profit pool is produced by the strongest segments.
