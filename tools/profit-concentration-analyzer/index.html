Profit Concentration Analyzer

This diagnostic measures how much of total gross profit comes from your top revenue segments. It estimates profit contribution per segment from revenue and gross margin.

In real businesses, a few product lines or customer groups often carry the margin while others absorb capacity. This shows where pricing power and cost structure actually produce cash generation.

Concentration matters because a narrow profit base makes forecasts fragile and forces reactive decisions. Diversified profit lets you allocate capacity and capital with more control.
