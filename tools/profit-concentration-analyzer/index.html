Profit Concentration Analyzer

This diagnostic evaluates how profit is distributed across the core revenue segments inside a business. Many companies monitor revenue by product line or customer group but rarely evaluate which activities actually generate the majority of economic profit.

Two segments may produce similar revenue while generating dramatically different profit contributions. Operators frequently discover that a small portion of the business quietly generates most of the profit, while other areas contribute little or even dilute overall performance.

Understanding where profit is structurally concentrated helps operators evaluate strategic risk, dependency on specific revenue drivers, and the resilience of the overall profit engine.
