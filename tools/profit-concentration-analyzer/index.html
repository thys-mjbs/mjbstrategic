Profit Concentration Analyzer

Businesses often evaluate performance by looking at total revenue growth. However, revenue alone rarely reveals where the real economic engine of the company sits. In many companies a small number of products, services, or customer groups generate a disproportionate share of total profit.

This diagnostic examines how profit is distributed across the main revenue segments of a business. By combining revenue and gross margin for each segment, the tool estimates the profit contribution generated by each activity and determines how dependent the business may be on its strongest profit drivers.

Operators typically encounter this issue when a business appears healthy on a revenue basis but becomes vulnerable when one key product line weakens or a major customer changes behaviour. Understanding how concentrated profit generation is across the organisation helps leadership evaluate structural resilience and exposure to disruption.
