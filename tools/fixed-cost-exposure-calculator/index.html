Fixed Cost Exposure Calculator

This diagnostic measures how exposed your profit is to fixed operating costs at current revenue levels. It calculates break-even revenue and margin of safety using your variable cost ratio.

In real operations, fixed costs like rent and salaries do not shrink when orders slow down. When revenue drops, the same fixed base consumes a larger share of contribution margin and profitability collapses quickly.

This matters because cost structure determines how aggressively you can price, hire, and allocate capacity. It also defines how much revenue decline you can absorb before cash generation turns negative.

Use average revenue from the last normal trading month.

Include rent, salaries, insurance, and other unavoidable overhead.

Percent of revenue consumed by direct costs and fulfilment.

This tool assumes your internal reporting can separate fixed costs from variable costs and track monthly revenue consistently. If those numbers are blended across accounts, the diagnostic will show directionally correct pressure but not the true structural exposure.
