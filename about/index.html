About MJB Strategic

MJB Strategic reflects over two decades of hands-on financial and operational leadership across retail, eCommerce, hospitality, and multi-entity commercial environments.

I have carried responsibility for annual revenues approaching nine figures in local currency and managed balance sheets in the tens of millions. These were inventory-heavy, margin-sensitive businesses where cash flow, procurement discipline, pricing control, and operational execution directly determined stability and growth.

What the work actually is

Each engagement is a defined financial diagnostic focused on isolating how profit, capital, cost structure, inventory, supplier exposure, and demand interact inside a business.

Some engagements identify structural strain before it escalates. Others identify leverage and opportunity before capital is committed. The objective is the same in both cases to surface economic reality clearly enough that decision-making improves immediately.

The underlying capability

My background includes multi-entity management accounts, budgeting and forecasting, scenario modelling, pricing analysis, direct international sourcing, inventory control, SOP design, automation, and financial reporting aligned to executive decision cycles.

I have built reporting frameworks where none existed, implemented procurement strategies that improved margin, launched and scaled digital revenue channels, and restored clarity in businesses navigating transition or ownership change.

The consistent thread has been structural financial thinking. Seeing how the pieces connect and translating that complexity into something precise and usable for business owners.

How this approach was formed

Across different industries and ownership structures, the pattern has remained consistent. When financial information is technically correct but structurally unclear, decision-making becomes reactive.

When profit drivers are isolated, capital strain is quantified, and breakpoints are tested, decision quality improves even before operational changes are made. That principle forms the foundation of MJB Strategic.

Who this is for

This work is designed for owner-led businesses operating at meaningful scale who either feel structural pressure building or are actively seeking to move on clear, quantified opportunity.

Operators managing real revenue, real supplier exposure, real inventory, and real financial responsibility. Some arrive under pressure. Others arrive preparing for expansion. Both require clarity before committing capital or restructuring direction.
