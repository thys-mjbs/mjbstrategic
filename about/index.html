About MJB Strategic

MJB Strategic exists to give owner-led businesses structural financial clarity. The work is designed for operators who are carrying real economic weight and want to see what is true inside the numbers before making decisions.

Some clients engage because pressure is building. Others engage because the business is stable and they want to move with precision. The diagnostic process is the same: isolate what is driving outcomes, where risk is accumulating, and where opportunity is being missed because it is not visible in the current view.

What the work actually is

Each engagement is a defined financial diagnostic. You engage for one domain or multiple, depending on what is happening in the business.

The output is analytical clarity, delivered as a written report, a walkthrough session, or both. The work explains what is happening, why it is happening, and where economic weight sits. Execution remains with the business owner and their existing advisors.

How this approach was built

This methodology was formed inside operating businesses where financial and operational reality had to be reconciled under real constraints. The focus has consistently been on decision usefulness, not reporting cosmetics.

The pattern is consistent: when the structure is visible, decision quality improves immediately. That holds whether the business is trying to stabilise, expand, restructure, or deploy capital more intelligently.

About the operator

MJB Strategic is run by Thys Benson. He has operated inside hospitality and retail businesses, with experience intersecting financial services and capital-intensive environments. His largest commercial responsibility sat in the high eight figures in local currency annual revenue, with corresponding mid eight-figure balance sheet oversight.

The work is built around practical analysis that owners can use. No posturing, no generic playbooks, no theatre. Just a clean explanation of what is driving results and what that implies for risk and opportunity.

Who this is for

Owner-led businesses operating at meaningful scale, where complexity has outgrown intuitive oversight. Businesses that want analytical precision before committing capital, changing structure, or chasing growth.

Some arrive under pressure. Some arrive preparing for opportunity. The common requirement is the same: clarity before action.
